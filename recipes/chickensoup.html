Chicken Soup

Bundle up! Its chicken soup season

Ingredients:

  • Chicken
  • Chicken Broth
  • Chicken Bullion
  • Johnny's Seasoning
  • Wide Egg Noodles

How to Make:

  1. Cook your chicken
  2. Shred the chicken
  3. Put your crockpot on high setting
  4. Place your chicken in crockpot
  5. Pour 2 cartons of chicken broth into crockpot
  6. Add 2 tablespoons of chicken bullion
  7. Let crockpot cook for 4 hours
  8. Add in egg noodles, wait 30 minutes

    9. Bon Appetit
