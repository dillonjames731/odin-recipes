Fried Pickles

One of my favorite ball game snacks, these are fantastic with a side of ranch!

Ingredients:

  • Pickles
  • Eggs
  • Flour
  • Johnny's Seasoning
  • oil

How to Make:

  1. Put egg in bowl, and whisk
  2. 1 cup of flour into a bowl
  3. 2 tablespoons of Johnny's seasnoning, added to flour, mix.
  4. Dip pickle into egg, and then into flour, and one last dip into egg
  5. Add 3 cups of oil to sauce pan, heat for 3 minutes on medium
  6. Put pickle in oil for 2 minutes
  7. Place cooked pickle on paper towel

    8. Bon Appetit
